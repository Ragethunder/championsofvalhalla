Berzerker

One of a band of ancient Norse warriors legendary for their savagery and reckless frenzy in battle. Berzerker fights with an uncontrollable, trance-like fury and is a fearsome opponent in close combat.
Berzerkers are strong and heavily armored, making them perfect chargers.

Notable abilities:

  • Charge

    Instead of running toward it's target, this unit will charge it furiously and stun the target for 1 move.

  • Leap Slam

    This unit picks a random target and leaps onto it, slaming the ground and damaging target for 100% weapon damage and all other targets for 50% weapon damage.

  • Giant's Strength

    Passive: This unit deals 25% more damage with its normal attacks and takes 30% less damage from enemies.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.

Reaver

Reavers are fierce warriors that rely on their skills with various weapons and their speed to overcome even the toughest of enemies. They strike so fast and acurately that enemies have no time to react and avoid attacks, making them an easy prey for Reaver. They might not be the bulkiest of the northern warriors, but their skill is legendary.

Notable abilities:

  • Double Strike

    Strikes the target twice in one turn.

  • Critical Strike

    A heavy blow that deals 150% weapon damage and causes the target to bleed for 10% of current health per turn for 3 turns.

  • Parry

    Passive: This unit has a 25% chance to evade enemy's attack and strike back for 75% of normal attack's damage.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.

Scout

Scouts are extremely quick and deadly fighters. They are masters of disguise and deception and can disable targets from long distances using bow and arrow. Despite their lack of heavy armor or strength, scouts can easily dodge attacks and kill large targets with a single deadly strike.

Notable abilities:

  • Backstab

    Passive: If this unit was not being focused by the target in last turn, this unit will deal 75% more damage to the target.

  • Poison Arrow

    This unit fires an arrow from a long distance, dealing 100% weapon damage and stunning the target for 1 turn.

  • Quickness

    Passive: This unit can travel fast, losing no turn points when switching targets.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.

Ranger

Rangers are terrifying opponents, known for their accuracy with bow and arrow, as well as their knowledge of seidr. They can shoot enemies from large distances with deadly precision and take cover when approached by heavy targets.

Notable abilities:

  • Ranged Attacks

    Passive: This unit can attack targets without approaching them, but deals 20% reduced damage.

  • Force Push

    Using seidr, this unit can push targets away, stunning them for 1 move.

  • Disengage

    Passive: When approached by enemy, this unit will retreat to safety.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.

Volva

Volva is an ancient female shaman that can use the power of seidr, gifted to her from Gods to wreak havoc on her enemies. Volva can also see into future, giving her an advantage over opponents and making her near invincible.

Notable abilities:

  • Fireball

    Using seidr, throws a ball of molten lava onto target, dealing 100% weapon damage to it and 50% weapon damage to other enemies.

  • Energy Shield

    Using seidr, creates a protective barrier around a friendly target that will absorb 2 enemy attacks.

  • Quick Reflexes

    Passive: This unit evades 33% of incoming attacks.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.

Druid

Druids are well known for their knowledge of seidr which they use to call upon forces of nature. They can heal allies, summon creatures and enchant their weapons to deal more damage.

Notable abilities:

  • Ghost Wolf

    Using seidr, summons a wolf companion to fight for you. Wolf's power is determined by summoner champion's level.

  • Enchant

    Using seidr, imbues a friendly target's weapon so that it deals 20% more damage and gives each attack a chance to heal the target for 30% of damage done.

  • Mend

    Passive: This unit takes 15% reduced damage, and when damaged, this unit will heal whole group for 25% of damage taken.

Note that these abilities are not restricted to this class, but it takes less time to acquire them if you pick this class.
